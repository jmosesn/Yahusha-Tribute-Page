Yahusha Hamashiach

A Tribute to the King of Kings

Alpha - Omega

Yahusha Hamashiach name meaning

Yahusha is Hebrew for Jesus, meaning "Yah is Salvation." Hamashiach is Hebrew for Christ, meaning "The Anointed One," or "The Messiah."

Also known as

Yeshua in Aramaic. Yahusha Hebrew name variants include; Yahshua, Yahushua.

Birth & Death

Circa Spring or Fall of 6-4 BC to Passover of AD 30-36

Birthplace

Bethlehem, from the Province of Judea

Hometown

Nazareth, from the Region of Galilee

Nationality

Israelite, from the Tribe of Judah

Family

The Genealogy of Yahusha: Matthew 1:1-17, Luke 3:23-37. Father - Joseph Mother - Mary Brothers - James, Joseph, Judas and Simon (Matthew 12:46 & 13:55, Mark 3:31 & 6:3, Luke 8:19) Sisters - Mentioned without names (Matthew 13:55-56 & Mark 6:3).

Occupation

Carpenter; one who work with his hands, and a Rabbi(Teacher).

Achievements

  • Preached the Basorah "good news" of the kingdom
  • Turned water into wine
  • Fed 5,000 people with 2 fish & 5 loaves of bread
  • Healed the sick, lame, & the blind
  • Walked on water
  • Calmed the sea storm
  • Casts out evil & unclean spirits
  • Raised people up from the dead
  • Lived a perfect life
  • Died for the penalty of our sins
  • Resurrected from the dead
  • forgives sins
  • Became the way to everlasting life

Summary

Yahusha Hamashiach lived in 1st-Century Palestine/Canaan/Israel during the time Israel was under Roman Empire rule. The Israelites were conquered and scattered into various nations, due to their wickedness towards Yahuah. Yahusha began His ministry at 30yrs old, preaching the gospel of the kingdom. He preached to Israel to repent, for the kingdom of Yah is near. He is often misrepresented as being only gentle and loving. Yahusha was a real man who always kept it real. He preached the goodnews to gather the lost sheep who had been scattered. He was a revolutionary. Not only was he a revolutionary leader & teacher amongst Israel, He was a perfect man. The Lamb of Yah who was without blemish, the Passover Lamb. He was persecuted off of false charges, led by the pharisees. He was severly torchered, beaten beyond recognition, nailed and hung on a tree. His death & ressurection brought salvation to Israel and to the world, to those who cling unto Him. He is the way, the truth, and the life. Let us all give honor and thanks to The Lion of Judah, the High Priest/King, our Lord & Savior. All Praise to the Most High Yah through His Son Yahusha Hamashiach!
